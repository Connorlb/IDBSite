Smores

  • Ingredients
    8 sheets honey graham crackers
    One 4.4-ounce milk chocolate bar
    Pinch kosher salt
    8 large marshmallows
  • Instructions
    Heat a grill to medium-low heat.
    Halve each graham cracker sheet crosswise into 2 squares.
    Working with 1 or 2 s'mores at a time, place a square of graham cracker on a piece of foil and top with a portion of chocolate and a pinch of kosher salt.
    Place on the edge of the grill just until the chocolate is warmed and softened, about 30 seconds.
    Take the graham cracker squares and chocolate to your grill.
    Working with a few at a time, skewer a marshmallow on a long fork or metal skewer and hold over the heat about 2 inches above the grates.
    Toast, turning occasionally, until the marshmallow puffs and turns golden brown, 1 to 2 minutes.
    Using the square of graham with the chocolate and a plain graham square, place the marshallow on the chocolate and, using the plain graham, squish the marshmallow down and pull off the skewer.
    Eat while it is warm and gooey and perfect.

  • Time to prepare: 10 minutes

  • Reviews: 5 Stars
